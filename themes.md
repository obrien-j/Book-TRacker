Option A — "Monster Collection"

5×6 grid of squares, each containing a unique friendly little monster (think googly eyes, silly grins, tiny horns)
Border is a parade of monsters marching around the edge
As your son reads each book, he colours in (or puts a sticker on) the monster's square
Tagline at the top: "Can you collect all 30 Book Monsters?"
Option B — "Space Explorer"

5×6 grid where each square is a planet or star
Border features rockets, astronauts, and alien friends floating around
Each book read = a new planet "discovered" (check it off or colour it in)
Tagline: "30 Books. 30 Planets. One Super Reader."
Option C — "Treasure Map"

6×5 grid laid out like a winding path on a treasure map
Squares are numbered stepping stones leading to a big treasure chest in the final square (#30)
Border has pirates, parrots, palm trees, and waves
Tagline: "Read your way to the treasure!"
Option D — "Under the Sea"

5×6 grid, each square has a different sea creature (octopus, starfish, whale, clownfish…)
Border is ocean waves along the top, coral reef along the bottom, seaweed on the sides
Tagline: "Dive into 30 books!"
